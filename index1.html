About Us

Our story & mission

Our Story

Established in 1982, SABIR TIKKA has been serving the finest BBQ and Fast Food in Hyderabad for over four decades. What started as a small family-run dhaba has now become one of the most trusted names in the city's food scene.

Our secret lies in our authentic recipes passed down through generations, using only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods.
